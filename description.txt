This SVG visualization represents a decade's worth of rainfall data of Charlestoen-Bunker Hill (Boston, MA), from 2014 to 2023, showcasing annual variations in rainfall measured in total inches. It provides a clear comparison of yearly fluctuations in the total inches of rainfall, highlighting the trends and changes over the specified period.

Note:
X-Axis: Represents the years from 2014 to 2023.
Y-Axis: Measures the total inches of rainfall, employing a scale that accommodates the dataset's range.
Graph Type: A line graph connects annual data points, illustrating the dynamic changes in rainfall over the ten-year span.

Dataset Source: https://www.bwsc.org/environment-education/rainfall-garden
